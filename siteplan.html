Damimah Junior School

Website Site Plan

Site Name

Damimah Junior School

This name was selected because it directly identifies the institution and clearly communicates to parents, students, and the community what the website represents. The word "Junior" helps distinguish the school level and makes it easy for visitors to know they are in the right place when searching for elementary education options.

Potential domain: damimahschool.edu or damimahjs.org

Site Purpose

The Damimah Junior School website serves as the primary digital platform for the school community. The site will provide:

  • Information about the school's mission, values, and academic programs
  • Admissions information and enrollment procedures for prospective families
  • A calendar of school events, activities, and important dates
  • Contact information for school administrators and staff
  • News and announcements relevant to current students and parents
  • Resources for parents to support their children's learning journey

Scenarios

Scenario 1

"What are the admission requirements and how do I enroll my child at Damimah Junior School?"

Parents looking to enroll their children need clear information about age requirements, application deadlines, required documents, and the enrollment process.

Scenario 2

"What extracurricular activities and programs does the school offer?"

Families want to know about sports, clubs, arts programs, and other activities available to students beyond regular classroom instruction.

Scenario 3

"How can I contact the school administration or my child's teacher?"

Parents and community members need easy access to contact information, including phone numbers, email addresses, and office hours.

Color Scheme

The following colors have been carefully selected to create a warm, welcoming, and professional appearance that appeals to both children and parents:

Primary Color: Deep Blue

Hex Code: #2C5F7C

Usage: Main headings, navigation bar, footer background, and primary buttons. This color conveys trust, stability, and professionalism.

Secondary Color: Warm Orange

Hex Code: #F4A460

Usage: Subheadings, accent elements, links, and call-to-action buttons. This warm color adds energy and friendliness to the design.

Background Color: Light Gray

Hex Code: #F9F9F9

Usage: Page background and section backgrounds to provide a clean, readable surface.

Text Color: Dark Gray

Hex Code: #333333

Usage: Body text and paragraphs for optimal readability.

Typography

Heading Font: Poppins

Font Family: 'Poppins', sans-serif

Usage: All headings (h1, h2, h3) throughout the website. This modern, geometric sans-serif font is friendly and highly legible, making it perfect for educational websites.

Weights Used: 600 (Semi-Bold) for main headings, 500 (Medium) for subheadings

Body Font: Open Sans

Font Family: 'Open Sans', sans-serif

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, lists, and general content. Open Sans is highly readable on screens and pairs well with Poppins.

Weight Used: 400 (Regular) for body text
