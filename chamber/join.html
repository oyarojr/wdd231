NP Membership Benefits

  • Access to chamber networking events
  • Monthly newsletter with business insights
  • Basic business directory listing
  • Access to member resources and guides
  • Community forums and discussion groups

Perfect for: Non-profit organizations looking to connect with the business community

Bronze Membership Benefits

  • All NP Membership benefits
  • Enhanced directory listing with logo
  • 10% discount on all chamber events
  • Access to exclusive workshops and seminars
  • Quarterly business development sessions
  • Networking breakfast invitations (4 per year)
  • Certificate of membership

Perfect for: Small to medium enterprises seeking growth opportunities

Silver Membership Benefits

  • All Bronze Membership benefits
  • Featured listing in member directory
  • 20% discount on all chamber events
  • 2 spotlight features on homepage per year
  • Priority access to business matchmaking
  • Monthly networking breakfast invitations
  • Business mentorship program access
  • Social media promotion (quarterly)
  • Free attendance to annual business summit

Perfect for: Established businesses wanting enhanced visibility

Gold Membership Benefits

  • All Silver Membership benefits
  • Premium directory listing with full profile
  • 30% discount on all chamber events
  • 4 spotlight features on homepage per year
  • Exclusive gold member networking events
  • Speaking opportunities at chamber events
  • Dedicated account manager
  • Priority booth placement at trade shows
  • Monthly social media promotion
  • VIP seating at annual gala and awards
  • Business consultation services (quarterly)
  • Logo featured on chamber website and materials

Perfect for: Leading enterprises seeking maximum exposure and influence
